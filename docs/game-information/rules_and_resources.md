Rules and resources

Base resources:

C = Currency / coin. General monetary and upkeep resource used by most structures and units.
F = Food. Used for upkeep and sustenance.
W = Wood. Common construction material.
O = Ore. Basic metal and industrial input.
M = Mushrooms. Special biological resource, often associated with swamp output and some advanced production.
H = Hydrogen. Important for advanced industry and air/naval development.

Advanced resources:

RM = Refined metal.
FS = Fovium steel.
CB = Carbon bombs.
SS = Supersteel.
AM = Antimatter.
AD = AI cores / advanced droids component, depending on unit context.
URM = Ultra refined metal.
DM = Dark matter.
CFB = Condensed fuel blocks.
CGM = Compressed gravitic material.
EC = Exotic core.
OS = Omega shard.
KRM = Kingdom resource material, salvage, or strategic war gain as awarded by advanced unit effects.

General resource notes:

Resources are generated from terrain, structures, units, and other systems as specified by game rules.
Storage limits are constrained by stockpiles and market capacity.
Some unit deaths transfer resources to the enemy.
Some structures or special units generate yearly income directly.

Upkeep:

UPK entries define what must be paid to maintain a unit or structure.
If upkeep cannot be sustained, the unit or structure may be considered inactive, degraded, or otherwise subject to admin handling according to the broader game rules.

Trading:

Trade routes unlock transport and player-to-player trade.
Markets and trade schools expand your practical trade capacity.

Terrain production recap:

Forest provides wood.
Mountains provide ore.
Swamp provides mushrooms.
Water provides food.
Other production may come from race features, structures, or special assets.

Technology progression:

Higher-tier units and structures require more advanced resources.
Specialized armaments, nuclear capitals, and the most powerful vehicles rely on rare materials and long build investment.
